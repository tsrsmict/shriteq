Put your creativity and ingenuity to the test in the hackathon, as you build what could be the next billion-dollar tech startup!

Participating teams will build a technological product based on a given theme. The product can be either software based, such as an app, website, or AR/VR; or hardware based.


Format

The hackathon will consist of an online preliminary round, in which participants will submit a proof of concept of their idea. Five teams will be shortlisted for offline finals, in which they will present their finished product to a panel of judges onstage. The preliminary round will take place over Devpost. Participants will be expected to submit a working project along with essential details about their submission.
The preliminary round will take over Devpost. The Devpost link for the hackathon will be posted here soon.


Evaluation Criteria for the Finals

  • Problem area addressed and potential impact
  • Technical accomplishment and innovation
  • Financial model and viability
  • User interface and experience
  • Creativity

Timeline

  • 23rd September - The prompt for the preliminary round is released
  • 30th September - Preliminary round submissions over DevPost
  • 5th October - Teams shortlisted for the finals are revealed
  • 12th October - In-person finals at The Shri Ram School, Moulsari
