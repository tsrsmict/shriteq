Race to solve as many programming questions as you can in one hour!

  • This event will test the logical and programming abilities of participants.
  • Participants will have one hour to solve as many programming challenges as they can on HackerRank.
  • Participants will write code based on a problem description. Their program which will be automatically tested by an autograder.
  • Participants will be ranked by the number of questions solved, and ties will be broken by the time taken to solve questions.
  • Languages allowed: Python and C++.
  • The event will consist of an online preliminary round and offline final round.
  • The top 10 participants in the preliminary round will advance to offline finals at TSRS Moulsari.
  • During the preliminary round, it will be mandatory for participants to have their cameras and microphones turned on. They will also be required to screen share.

Timeline

  • Saturday, 1st October - Online preliminary round over Discord at 11 AM
  • Tuesday, 11th October - Final offline round. Qualifying participants report at 8 AM
