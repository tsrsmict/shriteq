ShriTeq’s most awaited event returns! The quiz will test contestants’ knowledge of computers, gadgets, tech companies, space technology, and much more.

There will be separate events for junior (grade 5), middle (grades 6-8), and senior (grades 9-12) teams from each school. Each school may send only one team of 3 students each for each category.

All teams will first be invited to the Moulsari campus for a preliminary round. 12 teams will be selected from each category for semi-finals. Teams will advance up to the finals, which will be conducted by NDTV’s ‘Gadget Guru’ Mr. Rajiv Makhni.


Timeline

  • 3rd October - Teams invited to TSRS Moulsari for offline preliminaries
  • 10th October - Offline semi-finals for the middle and senior quizzes
  • 11th October - Offline semi-finals and finals for the junior quiz
  • 12th October - Offline finals for the middle and senior quizzes
