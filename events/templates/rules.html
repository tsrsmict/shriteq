Rules

  • Any one student from a school will be allowed to participate in:
    • One online event
    • One offline event or hybrid event
    • Unlimited open events
  • In case of any clashes between event timings, no concessions will be made for participants wishing to participate in multiple events.
  • Event details are subject to change. Please visit www.shriteq.org for the latest information on all events.

  • Decisions taken by the OC and the judging panel for any individual event will be final and binding.
